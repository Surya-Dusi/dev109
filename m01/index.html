Chicken Marbella

Chicken Marbella is a classic chicken recipe that is easy to make by marinating chicken peices overnight with sweet prunes, olives, garlic, and red wine vinegar. Baste the chicken ith the pan jucies as it bakes for amazing flavor. It's a perfect party dish, and keeps and reheats well.

Recipe 2: Pizza Lava Toast

A simple toast covered with melty cheese toppings has been making the rounds on social media, but it's not just a slice of bread with some cheese melted on top. To give this toast the "lava" in its name, you actually stack two pieces of toast together to make a combination of grilled cheese and pizza toast.
